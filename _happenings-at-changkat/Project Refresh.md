### Upcoming: 6 May 2023 (Sat) ###

The main organisers of Project Refresh is Young NTUC and North East CDC, aiming to improve the living environment of seniors and low income families through a series of refurbishment works such as painting, cleaning and decluttering.

Upcoming, Tampines Changkat is working with the organisers to bring this meaningful event to our division as part of the 20th Anniversary of the initiative.

<h2>Our Belief: A little kindness goes a long way</h2>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/IMG_4646.jpg">
</div>
<p>Joy Box is initiated by Tampines Changkat in 2020 to spread joy to those
around us who are less privileged. During festive seasons, we hope to spread
joy to seniors, families and individuals, some of whom may be going through
a tough time. Over past few editions, we have delivered over 7,000 joy
boxes our beneficiaries from Tampines Changkat, hospices, hospitals, women’s
shelters, and children’s homes receive gifts lovingly prepared by all our
generous donors. 🙌✨ &nbsp;</p>
<p>It was truly touching for their invaluable support in spreading joy to
those who need it most this season. 🎄🤝 Together, we’ve made this season
brighter for so many! 🌟❤️</p>
<p>Look out for the upcoming Joy Box updates on our <a href="https://www.facebook.com/tampines.changkat" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Facebook</a>!</p>
